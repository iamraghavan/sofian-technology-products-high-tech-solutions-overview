Careers

Our vision of the future is a world where the right information moves people forward. How we reach this future is through our daily mission to put the right information in the right hands at the right time and help people to make the best possible decisions.

Working together towards a bright future

If you want to put your talent to work, be rewarded for your efforts and creativity, and be part of a great team, you should talk with us. At Sofian, we are delivering solutions that answer real needs – and our partners and customers tell us that every day.

Career at Sofian Technology

When you work at Sofian, you are part of a team. A team with a bold vision of the future and the important part we have to play in achieving it - as an organization and as individuals.

Working worldwide, no matter where you're located, we could have that perfect opportunity that will bring out the best in you. From software development to clinical consulting, sales to customer support and training, we have a range of jobs available.

We are always looking to recruit the wireless industry’s best and brightest. Let us know if you would like to join our team. As new positions become available, we will post them on this page. If you don’t want to wait to tell us about yourself – and in anticipation of future opportunities – send your resume to info@sofiantechnology.com.

Together we can make a difference in the lives of the people who benefit from our technology.
